The Absurd Beauty of The Earth

Earth is a unique artist; many photographs say so. However, most of them consist of the usual mountains or saturated shores. Sure, they are pleasant to the eye, but other paintings of the Earth could use some extra attention. Here, you will see and learn about new, refreshing, otherworldly and absurd landscapes, the abstract artworks of the Earth.
